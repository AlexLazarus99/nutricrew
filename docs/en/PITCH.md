# NutriCrew

**Team nutrition battles inside Telegram.**  
Photo → AI → points for your crew → weekly wars with real prizes.

---

## The problem

Calorie apps are **solo, boring, and easy to quit**. MyFitnessPal and KAVA track food — but **90% churn** because motivation dies alone. Corporate wellness budgets grow, yet nothing sticks in daily group chats where people actually live.

## The solution

**NutriCrew** = AI meal scan + **Duolingo-style team leagues** + **Telegram-native distribution**.

| Step | Experience |
|------|------------|
| 📸 Snap food | AI returns calories in ~3 sec |
| ⚡ Earn points | Personal streak + team multiplier |
| 🏆 Win the week | Leaderboard, Stars prizes, bragging rights |

Habit is built by **social pressure**, not willpower: skip a day → your team loses its bonus.

## Why now

- ✅ **AI food vision works** — no science risk; KAVA proved the scan loop  
- ✅ **Telegram Mini Apps** — 900M+ users, zero install friction, viral group chats  
- ✅ **Team games work** — Duolingo Leagues, Wordle, Strava segments  
- ✅ **Monetization gap** — nobody monetizes nutrition in TG with a **social pay model**
